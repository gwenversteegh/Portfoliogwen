Projects

Avocado media

I did my graduation internship at Avocado media, now known as Clarity. One of their problems was that they grew fast and really needed new people added to their team. To achieve this, they needed a career page on their own website. To find out what people want to see on a career page and what makes them apply for jobs, I did a little research project and implemented this on the page. I used their brand style in combination with my own creativity to make it easy accessible and make it a whole with the rest of the site.

KPMG Sofy Suite

KPMG Sofy Suite was quite a difficult site to navigate and to find what you're looking for. To make this easier I made a re-design from the original website. This was quite a challenge because there was a lot of information that needed to be shown.

About me

Hi! I'm Gwen Versteegh. I recently completed my studies in Communication and Multimedia Design, specializing in UX and UI design. I did a minor in frontend design & development but my passion lies in creating intuitive and engaging user experiences that matter to diverse audiences. Alongside my professional interests, I have a deep love for working with children, which adds a rewarding dimension to my life. In my free time, I give surf lessons to children with a mental disorder. I enjoy surfing,photography, pole dancing, or diving into a good book. These hobbies keep me active and inspired, and I always enjoy trying new things.
